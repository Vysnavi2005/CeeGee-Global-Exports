Company Profile

CeeGee Global Exports – "Beyond Borders! Beyond Expectations!" is a professionally managed Indian import–export company dedicated to delivering premium quality agricultural and food products to global markets with reliability, transparency, and international standards.

Under its flagship food brand Melano Food & Products, a registered member of the Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA), the company specializes in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-quality spices, rice (Basmati and Non-Basmati), pulses, grains, dehydrated items, and other value-added food products.

With strict quality control, hygienic processing, export-standard packaging, and efficient logistics management, CeeGee Global Exports ensures compliance with global trade regulations while building long-term partnerships by consistently exceeding customer expectations across international markets.
